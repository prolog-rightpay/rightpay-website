Privacy Policy for RightPay

Effective Date: November 10, 2023

Thank you for choosing RightPay! This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. Please take a moment to review this policy carefully.

By using the RightPay mobile application ("the App"), you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the App.

  1. Information We Collect:

    1. User Location:

      • Purpose: The App uses your location data to provide localized services, including finding nearby businesses.
      • Data Handling: Your location information is securely transmitted to Google for the sole purpose of identifying local businesses. We do not store this location data.

    2. Credit Card Information:

      • Purpose: RightPay tracks your credit card rewards, and for that purpose, we collect and store the first six digits of your credit card.
      • Data Handling: Only the first six digits of your credit card are requested and stored on our secure servers. We do not handle or store the full credit card details.

    3. IP Addresses:

      • Purpose: We collect and store IP addresses for security and analytical purposes.
      • Data Handling: IP addresses associated with your login sessions are securely stored and used for internal purposes only.

  2. How We Use Your Information:

    1. User Location:

      • Your location data is used solely for the purpose of identifying nearby businesses. We do not share this information with third parties.

    2. Credit Card Information:

      • The first six digits of your credit card are used exclusively for determining your credit card rewards within the RightPay App. We do not share this information with third parties.

    3. IP Addresses:

      • IP addresses are collected to enhance the security of your account and for analytical purposes. They are not shared with third parties.

  3. Data Security:

    • We take the security of your information seriously. RightPay employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

  4. Third-Party Services:

    • RightPay utilizes Google for location services. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

  5. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

    • RightPay reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the App or via email.

  6. Contact Us:

    • If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at business@harrystanton.com.
